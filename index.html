THE LATEST ON CTE

Keep up with all things Cage the Elephant including new releases, tour dates, festival appearances and more!

Forecastle Music Festival 2020 Cancelled!

The Forecastle Music Festival meant to run from July 17-19th 2020 in Louisville Kentucky and feature Cage the Elephant has now officially been cancelled due to growing COVID-19 fears. Tickets are reportedly being automatically refunded and event organziers are assuring event-goers that Forecastle will return in 2021.

2020 UK/European Tour Announced!

Cage the Elephant has announced their first UK/EU tour since 2017. They are set to play at the O2 Victoria Warehouse in Manchester on Wednesday 02/19 to kick it off. This is in the wake of their recently cancelled 2019 tour as a result of guitarist Nick Bockrath suffering from a break in his knee.

CTE Set to Play at FORECASTLE Music Festival

The band is currently set to play at the FORECASTLE music festival in Louisville, Kentucky. The festival runs from July 17 to July 19 at the Waterfront Park. The festival's FAQs can be found HERE.

New Album Social Cues Out Now! (4/19)

The new studio album by Cage the Elephant, titled Social Cues, has been officially released. You can buy now from major retailers or listen on Spotify.
